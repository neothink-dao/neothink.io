# Neothink+ User Guide

## Overview

Explore four platforms: Hub for achievement, Ascenders for prosperity, Neothinkers for happiness, Immortals for longevity.

## Routes

### Hub (go.neothink.io)
- `/dashboard`: Your central hub.
- `/discover`: Find new paths.
- `/onboard`: Start your journey.
- `/progress`: Track your growth.
- `/endgame`: Master your goals.

### Ascenders (joinascenders)
- `/ascender`: Prosperity overview.
- `/ascension`: Growth stages.
- `/flow`: Business templates.
- `/ascenders`: Community hub.

### Neothinkers (joinneothinkers)
- `/neothinker`: Happiness dashboard.
- `/neothink`: Core concepts.
  - `/revolution`: Neothink revolution.
  - `/fellowship`: Community fellowship.
  - `/movement`: Join the movement.
  - `/command`: Leadership structure.
- `/mark-hamilton`: Founder's insights.
- `/neothinkers`: Community hub.

### Immortals (joinimmortals)
- `/immortal`: Longevity dashboard.
- `/immortalis`: Immortalis projects.
- `/project-life`: Life extension resources.
- `/immortals`: Community hub.

## Getting Started
1. Sign up at neothink.io.
2. Log in and pick your platform(s).
3. Use the routes to explore.
