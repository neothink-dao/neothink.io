# Neothink+ User Guide for Test Users

## Overview

Welcome to the Neothink+ ecosystem! Our integrated platforms are designed to enhance different aspects of your life:

- **Hub (go.neothink.io)**: The central integration point for all Neothink platforms
- **Ascenders (joinascenders.org)**: Focused on personal prosperity and financial freedom
- **Neothinkers (joinneothinkers.org)**: Centered on achieving lasting happiness and integrated thinking
- **Immortals (joinimmortals.org)**: Dedicated to extending healthy lifespan and longevity

## Getting Started

1. Sign up at neothink.io.
2. Log in and subscribe to your chosen platform(s):
   - Ascenders
   - Neothinkers
   - Immortals
   - Hub access is included with any subscription
3. Use the routes below to explore the content and features.

## Routes

### Hub (go.neothink.io)

- **/dashboard**: View your overall progress and activities across all subscribed platforms
- **/discover**: Explore the different Neothink platforms and their core concepts
- **/onboard**: Complete the onboarding process with guided tutorials
- **/progress**: Track your advancement through all courses and materials
- **/endgame**: Master-level content and advanced integrations (requires achievement milestones)

### Ascenders (joinascenders.org)

- **/ascender**: Personal dashboard with prosperity metrics and progress
- **/ascension**: Step-by-step stages of financial independence
- **/flow**: Ready-to-use templates for value creation
- **/ascenders**: Community forum for Ascenders members

### Neothinkers (joinneothinkers.org)

- **/neothinker**: Personal dashboard for tracking integrated thinking progress
- **/neothink**: Core concepts of the Neothink system, with subroutes:
  - **/neothink/revolution**: Breaking free from mysticism and static thinking
  - **/neothink/fellowship**: Building connections with like-minded individuals
  - **/neothink/movement**: Understanding the worldwide Neothink movement
  - **/neothink/command**: Mastering the language patterns for effectiveness
- **/mark-hamilton**: Insights and teachings from the founder
- **/neothinkers**: Community forum for Neothinkers members

### Immortals (joinimmortals.org)

- **/immortal**: Personal dashboard with longevity metrics and progress
- **/immortalis**: Featured projects in longevity research and practice
- **/project-life**: Resources and tools for extending healthy lifespan
- **/immortals**: Community forum for Immortals members

## Community Features

Join discussions on the community pages for each platform:
- Ascenders: /ascenders
- Neothinkers: /neothinkers
- Immortals: /immortals

Each community page includes:
- Discussion forums organized by topic
- Member profiles and activity feeds
- Content sharing capabilities
- Direct messaging with other members

## Meetings and Events

View upcoming events and register for participation:
1. Navigate to any platform's dashboard
2. Click on the "Events" section
3. Browse upcoming meetings, workshops, and seminars
4. Register to receive Zoom links and calendar invitations
5. Set reminders and add to your personal schedule
