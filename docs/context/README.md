# Neothink Network State Ecosystem

This document serves as the source of truth for the core concepts and structure of the Neothink Network State Ecosystem.

## Core Platforms

### Ascenders ($99/month)
- **Access:** Ascension + FLOW + Ascenders Community
- **Value:** Greater PROSPERITY through value creation
- **Goal:** "Become wealthier and your wealthiest"
- **Components:**
  - Ascension Business System (5-stage growth)
  - FLOW (GoHighLevel white label platform)
  - Weekly Q&A/Office Hours
  - Affiliate sales training
  - Weekly business training

### Neothinkers ($99/month)
- **Access:** Neothink + Mark Hamilton + Neothinkers Community
- **Value:** Greater HAPPINESS through integrated thinking
- **Goal:** "Become happier and your happiest"
- **Components:**
  - Neothink University (40+ courses)
  - 12-level progression system
  - 7 study areas
  - 10 specialty institutes
  - Weekly implementation sessions

### Immortals ($99/month)
- **Access:** Immortalis + Project Life + Immortals Community
- **Value:** Greater LONGEVITY through self-leadership
- **Goal:** "Become healthier and your healthiest"
- **Components:**
  - Prime Law framework
  - Project Life supplements
  - Anti-aging research
  - Health optimization resources
  - Digital country structure
